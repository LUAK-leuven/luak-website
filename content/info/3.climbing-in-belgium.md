There are many rock climbing areas in Belgium. But before you take your climbing gear and head out to explore them there is some practical stuff to take into account.

## ⛰ About climbing on Belgian rocks

Belgium is a strange country. For legal reasons to **climb Belgian rock**, you must be a **member of the [KBF](https://www.klimenbergsportfederatie.be)** (the flemish climbing federation) or any other **[UIAA](https://www.theuiaa.org) climbing federation**. (You can check whether your climbing federation is a member of the UIAA over [here](https://www.theuiaa.org/members-directory/)).

If you become KBF member you'll have to select a club. If you chose LUAK in that list you'll receive 5€ discount on your membership. Visit their website at: <http://www.klimenbergsportfederatie.be/>

Note that for our initiations you don't need to be a KBF member, we arrange a day insurance for the whole group.
