### I'm no longer a student, can I still join LUAK?

Yes ofcourse! There is no age limit for becoming a member, we also have a lot of non-student members.

### How do I get my membership card?

Once you created an account and paid your membership fee you can pass by on a club evening (Thursday evening starting from 20h30) to pick up your membership card.
